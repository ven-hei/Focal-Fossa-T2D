Welcome

A prototype database for understanding the evolutionary genetic basis of Type 2 Diabetes.

Our Mission

This project integrates genomic, functional, and clinical data to provide insights into how evolutionary processes have shaped T2D susceptibility in different South Asian populations.

About us

Data Sources

We use data from leading research sources including Ensembl, T2D Knowledge Portal, NCBI and GWAS Catalog.

Key Features

  • Retrieve genetic variant data for T2D.
  • Analyze selection metrics in South Asian populations.
  • Visualize genomic data with interactive plots.
  • Download summary statistics for further research.
